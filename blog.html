Enactus National Competition 2025

Posted on June 10, 2025 by Adu precious

The most anticipated youth event of the year. The Enactus National Competition 2025. Witness the spirit of innovation and impact as top teams in Nigeria battle for national glory. Join us to champion solutions that empower communities and shape the future.

Back live and stronger than ever, The Enactus National Competition returns in its full-fledged in-person glory. This year brings a powerful display of impactful initiatives, driven by the next generation of entrepreneurs and leaders.

Students from universities across Nigeria will showcase their social impact projects, demonstrating how they've used entrepreneurial action to create positive change in their communities. From sustainable agriculture solutions to technology innovations that bridge the digital divide, these projects represent the future of social entrepreneurship in Nigeria.

The competition will feature multiple rounds of presentations, where teams will be evaluated on their project's impact, sustainability, and potential for scalability. Expert judges from the business world, academia, and development sector will assess each presentation, providing valuable feedback to the participating students.

Beyond the competition itself, the event serves as a networking platform where students can connect with potential mentors, investors, and like-minded peers. Workshops and panel discussions will be held throughout the event, covering topics such as social entrepreneurship, impact measurement, and funding opportunities for social ventures.

The winning team will represent Nigeria at the Enactus World Cup, competing against teams from over 36 countries. This global platform offers an incredible opportunity for Nigerian students to showcase their innovations on the world stage and potentially secure international partnerships and funding.

Registration is now open for spectators and participants. Don't miss this opportunity to witness the future leaders of Nigeria in action and be inspired by their dedication to creating a better world through entrepreneurial action.
